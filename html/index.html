Homepage

Restaurants

Information about restaurants that have been/will be reviewed. Restaurants may be viewed and added.


Restaurant Cuisines

A table that shows cuisines alongside the restaurants that serve them. A single restaurant could have multiple cuisines. (ex.: A restaurant can serve both Indian and vegetarian cuisines) Relationships may be viewed, added, updated, and deleted.


Cuisines

This table shows the different categories of cuisine that a restaurant may serve. Cuisines may be viewed and added.


Price Levels

Categories of prices that reflect ranges, in dollars, of what a single diner can expect to pay for a meal. Price levels can be viewed and added.


Reviews

Records of the reviews that have been completed by reviewers. Each review is for a specific restaurant but each restaurant can have multiple reviews. Reviews can be viewed, added, and updated.


Reviewers

Personal information about the individual reviewers as may be needed by the business. Reviewers can be viewed and added.


Expenses

This table records the expenses that a reviewer can incur as a result of a review that requires reimbursement. This could include dining bills, parking, mileage, etc. Expenses can be viewed and added.
